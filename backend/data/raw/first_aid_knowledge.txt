Snake Bite First Aid

Keep the patient calm and still.
Immobilize the bitten limb.
Remove tight clothing or jewelry.
Do not cut the wound.
Do not suck the venom.
Seek medical help immediately.

Source: WHO First Aid Guidelines

---------------------------------

CPR Emergency

Check breathing.
Call emergency services.
Begin chest compressions.
Push hard and fast in the center of the chest.

Source: American Heart Association

---------------------------------

Burn Treatment

Cool the burn with clean running water for at least 10 minutes.
Remove tight items like rings.
Cover with sterile dressing.

Source: Red Cross First Aid Manual
